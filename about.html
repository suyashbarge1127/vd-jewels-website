Who We Are

VD Jewels Artison Pvt Ltd

Established over 35 years ago, VD Jewels Artison Pvt Ltd is a premier diamond and gemstone fine jewellery house based in Mumbai. We are renowned as the "Jeweller to the Jewellers," crafting exquisite collections for some of the world's most prestigious brands, including SENCO, GIVA, and Prince Jewellery.

Under the leadership of Director & GIA Gemologist **Yash Dangayach**, we combine traditional hand-mastery with state-of-the-art CAD/CAM innovation. Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that every piece of jewellery should be a legacy, meticulously crafted to perfection.

Our Vision

To redefine global luxury standards by providing unparalleled craftsmanship and setting new benchmarks in the fine jewellery industry.

Our Mission

To serve as the trusted manufacturing partner for luxury brands, delivering high-precision designs with absolute integrity and artistic excellence.

Our Infrastructure

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our primary 25,000 sq. ft., 8-storey manufacturing facility is located in SEEPZ, Mumbai, housing our master artisans and high-tech production lines. We also operate a secondary specialized plant in China to ensure global supply efficiency.
