LEADERSHIP

Vision shaped by experience

Our leadership team combines deep industry expertise with a strong focus on technology-driven innovation — shaping our vision and strategic direction across products and services.

Ilan Lavian

VISIONARY · 35+ YEARS

The visionary behind VD Jewels' remarkable success. With over 35 years of profound industry expertise, Ilan's global network of trusted partners solidifies VD Jewels as a premier diamond jewellery exporter. His ventures span fashion, bridal, and custom special orders for major North American retailers.

Vikas Dangayach

GROUP HEAD

At the helm of VD Jewels, Vikas brings decades of expertise with gemstones — particularly coloured stones — bringing a unique artistry to our pieces. His journey began with our SEEPZ unit and now spans an 8-storey, 20,000+ sq ft state-of-the-art facility housing the latest technology and finest skilled workforce.

Yash Dangayach

GIA Gemologist · Exports

A GIA gemologist rapidly growing the exports sector. His young creative bend coupled with financial intuitiveness makes him a rare combination for the future — keeping VD Jewels in step with digital marketing, innovative designs, and the needs of every clientele.

Eyal Lavian

Driving Force · VD Overseas

The eldest son of Ilan Lavian and the driving force for VD Overseas. Eyal lends the company a discerning outlook on designs, client requirements, and sales — blending Western and Indian sensibilities while keeping VD Jewels' core values intact.

Ronak Agarwal

DESIGN · FASHION ENTREPRENEURSHIP

A post-graduate in Fashion Entrepreneurship from London College of Fashion, Ronak has always had a creative eye for design. His youthful approach to jewellery design and client interactions adds a distinctive contemporary touch at VD Jewels.

Shrikant Karmakar

EXPORTS · SINCE INCEPTION

The driving force for exports of VD Jewels since its inception. His strong knowledge of jewellery production combined with sharp marketing strategy has led to lasting client relationships and rapid growth across sectors.

Ankit Jain

VICE PRESIDENT OF SALES

Bringing a wealth of expertise and a proven track record in luxury jewellery, Ankit oversees VD Jewels' commercial strategy — ensuring our commitment to craftsmanship is matched by world-class service. His passion for jewellery innovation is central to the VD family's continued success.
